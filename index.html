About
Located in California’s Sierra Nevada mountains, Yosemite is famous for its giant, ancient sequoia trees, iconic rock formations, and the granite cliffs of El Capitan and Half Dome. It’s the perfect destination to camping, rock climbing, and hiking, especially for families.

Tunnel View
Tunnel View is a must see for any first time visit to Yosemite. From this vista you can see El Capitan, Half Dome, and Bridalveil Falls. Best time to visit is during Sunrise and Sunset.
